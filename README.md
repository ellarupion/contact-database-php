# Contact-database-php 
Is a free online platform designed to help users manage their contact information. Users can easily input, store, and retrieve contact information, including full name, post, phone numbers, and comments. The site's intuitive interface and search functionality make it a convenient tool for personal or business use.

## Features:

- Add and edit contact information
- Search contacts by name, post, phone numbers, or comments
- User-friendly interface
- Simple and intuitive design

# How to use:

### Add a new contact:

- Click on the "Add" link in the navigation bar

- Fill out the contact form with the required information (full name, post, phone numbers, and comments)

- Click on "Submit" to save the contact

### Search for a contact:

- On the homepage, enter your search query in the search field

- Click on "Search" to retrieve the results

### Edit an existing contact:

- Search for the contact you wish to edit

- Click on the "Edit" button associated with the contact

- Update the contact information in the form

- Click on "Submit" to save the changes
